Frequently asked questions

What is Web3 Africa?
Web3 Africa is a high-impact initiative dedicated to building Africa's decentralized future. We focus on providing top-tier Web3 education, fostering a strong community of developers and creators, and connecting African talent with global opportunities in the blockchain space.
Who can join the SkillChain Program?
The SkillChain Program is designed for individuals across Africa who are passionate about learning Web3 technologies. While some programs may have prerequisites, we welcome beginners, developers, designers, and entrepreneurs who are eager to build a career in the decentralized ecosystem.
How can I become a partner?
We are always open to collaborating with organizations that share our vision for a decentralized Africa. If you're interested in partnering with us on events, education, or ecosystem development, please reach out to us via email at web3africa2024@gmail.com.
What kind of events do you host?
We host a variety of events, including technical workshops, developer bootcamps, campus meetups, and online AMA (Ask Me Anything) sessions with industry experts. Our goal is to provide continuous learning and networking opportunities for our community.
